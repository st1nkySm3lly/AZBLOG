New Year New Hazards

Jan 02 2024 6 min read

With the new year begining, that means peak has tumbled down and the storm has calmed for now. Amazon Thank my Driver has ended and more updates are soon to come for drivers such as their weather forcasting system and team for the unforeseeable conditions of nature, to even minor ajustments from driver feedback.

AMAZON: Thank My Driver Promotion Ended!

In the middle of December, Amazon once again introduced the Thank My Driver program, where drivers can earn a bounus by the end of the year. For every "Alexa, Thank my Driver" a customer comands in their Amazon app or Alexa, the driver for that specific delivery gets 5$ in compensation, to the update Amazon has made for every 1,000 thanks lets the driver earn an extra 100 dollars, with the top 5 thanked drivers who delivered between the 18th to the 31st gets them 10 thousand dollars each, even adding on the top 5 thanked drivers starting from the 12th of December (The start of the promotional event) will each get 25 thousand dollars and an additional 25 thousand dollars to be donated to a charity of their choice matched and paid by Amazon themselves. The event finally concluded on January 1st.

AMAZON: How we're helping delivery partners stay safe this winter!

As we expand further into the winter seasons and entering the early spring months, Amazon has stated that they have created a group of meteorologists to measure and record weather for drivers out on the roads, whether that be rain, snow, or any weather condition. Unfortunatly, they can not predict if you're gonna have a lucky day. They provide predictive forcasting technology that is then sent as a notification to all drivers in the area. An example would be that they would send a notice of severe thunderstorm in the Update section of the Amazon Flex App.

AMAZON: Fast, live chat now available for all Amazon Flex support topics!

Amazon has now introduced Live Chats, a faster and better method of finding out information and questions you may have while being on the road or even at home. You can get connected in the matter of 30 seconds, and issues resolved 2.5 timed faster than what it would take by email. If you are ever bored or have a question that should have been googled, Live chats are open from 5 a.m to 7 p.m PT for non-delivery questions.
